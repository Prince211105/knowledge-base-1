# Defining the Context Section in a Project Initiation Document (PID)

The context section explains why the project is being undertaken. Include the primary business drivers, the specific problem to solve, technical or strategic motivations, high‑level goals, success criteria, and measurable metrics. A concise mission statement aligns the team and keeps strategic objectives visible throughout the project lifecycle.

## Can Answer

- What elements should I include in the context section of a PID?
- How do I write a clear mission statement for a PID?

## Examples

- "The client aims to reduce checkout abandonment by 20% through a streamlined mobile experience."

## Warnings

- Omitting strategic context can lead to misaligned deliverables later.
