# Define Project Context for the PID

Context includes business drivers, problem statement, project purpose, technical vs. strategic motivations, business goals, success definition, and key metrics. A high‑level mission statement aligns the team and helps later decisions about scope changes.

## Can Answer

- What information belongs in the context section of a PID?
- How should business goals be documented in a PID?
- How do I define success criteria and metrics for a project?

## Examples

- Business goal: Increase online sales by 15% within 12 months.
- Metric: Conversion rate improvement measured via Google Analytics.

## Warnings

- Vague or missing context can cause misaligned expectations later.
