# Step 5 – Define Team Roles Using a RACI Matrix

The RACI matrix aligns expectations and governance:
- **Responsible**: Person(s) who execute the work.
- **Accountable**: Single person who makes final decisions and signs off.
- **Consulted**: Stakeholders whose input is required before proceeding.
- **Informed**: Parties kept up‑to‑date on progress.
Create a table with deliverables or activities on the left and all internal and external roles across the top. Assign one of the RACI letters to each intersecting cell, ensuring only one accountable per deliverable. Pair the matrix with a communication plan for additional detail.


## Can Answer

How do I create a RACI matrix in a Project Initiation Document to assign team responsibilities?

## Examples

- Deliverable: Final Design Mockups – Responsible: UX Designer, Accountable: Art Director, Consulted: Client PM, Informed: Development Lead.

## Warnings

- Assigning multiple Accountable persons creates confusion during sign‑off.
