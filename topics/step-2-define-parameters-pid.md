# Step 2: Define Project Parameters in the Project Initiation Document (PID)

The parameters section provides transparency on constraints and expectations. Include:
- Total budget and its breakdown (resource costs, tools, contingencies).
- Overall timeline with major milestones.
- Collaboration approach (communication tools, meeting cadence, client involvement).
- The initial target or MVP the team will focus on.
Clear parameters prevent misaligned expectations and help the team plan realistically.

## Can Answer

Which parameters should be documented in a Project Initiation Document (PID)?

## Examples

- Budget: $150,000 broken into $80k development, $40k design, $30k contingency.
- Timeline: 12 weeks with Phase 1 (Discovery) – 2 weeks, Phase 2 (Build) – 8 weeks, Phase 3 (QA) – 2 weeks.

## Warnings

- Do not hide budget constraints; hidden financial limits cause scope creep.
