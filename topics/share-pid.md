# Distribute the Project Initiation Document and Secure Stakeholder Sign‑off

Once the PID is complete, circulate it to all team members, new hires, and external stakeholders. Secure formal sign‑off from the client and internal decision‑makers, especially on assumptions and the RACI matrix. Store the PID in a shared repository for ongoing reference during status meetings and future onboarding.
