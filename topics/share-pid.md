# Distributing the Project Initiation Document and Securing Client Sign‑off

After completion, circulate the PID to the entire project team, new members, and the client. Obtain client sign‑off to formalize assumptions and the RACI. Store the document in a version‑controlled repository for future reference during check‑ins and stakeholder changes.

## Can Answer

- How should I share the PID with stakeholders?
- Do I need formal client sign‑off on the PID?

## Examples

- Upload the PID to the project’s SharePoint folder and send a notification email to all stakeholders.

## Warnings

- Not obtaining sign‑off can make assumptions ambiguous later.
