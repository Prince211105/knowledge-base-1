# Distributing and Signing Off the Project Initiation Document (PID)

After the PID is finalized, circulate it to the entire project team, new members, and the client. Secure client sign‑off to formalize assumptions and the RACI matrix. Store the signed PID in a version‑controlled repository (e.g., SharePoint, Git) to ensure easy access during check‑ins and stakeholder changes.

## Can Answer

- What is the recommended way to distribute the PID?
- How do I obtain and record client sign‑off on the PID?
- Where should I store the PID for version control?

## Examples

- Upload the PID to the project’s SharePoint folder and send a notification email to all stakeholders.

## Warnings

- Failing to obtain client sign‑off can make assumptions ambiguous later.
