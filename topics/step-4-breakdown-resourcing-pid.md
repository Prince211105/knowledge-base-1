# Step 4 – Build a Project Breakdown Structure (PBS) and Resource Allocation Plan

A Project Breakdown Structure (PBS) divides the project into work packages or phases. For each package, assign:
- Specific activities or tasks.
- Responsible team members or roles.
- Estimated effort (hours/days).
- Start and end dates (linked to the timeline).
The accompanying resource allocation plan shows who is allocated where, highlighting any overallocation or gaps. Running the draft through the team encourages feedback, uncovers hidden dependencies, and builds ownership.
