# Examples of Product, Service, and Process Work Breakdown Structures

Product WBS Example – Training Course: includes deliverable legs such as Training Materials, Resources, Advertising, Logistics, and Training Administration, decomposed to three levels (e.g., Work Books, Content, Course Location Rooms). Service WBS Example – Wedding Planning Service: includes Attire, Ceremony, Reception, Rehearsal Dinner, Bridal Lunch, Accommodations, Transportation; each leg may contain sub‑deliverables like Minister under Ceremony. Process WBS Example – Workplace Relocation Project: includes Current Location, Target Site Location, Relocation Staff, Transition Plan, Funding deliverables; some legs are further decomposed to Level 3 while others remain at Level 2 pending clarification.
