# Risks of Skipping the Project Initiation Document (PID)

Projects that begin without a PID frequently experience:
- Misaligned understanding of client needs.
- Frequent clarification requests and duplicated tickets.
- Unclear decision‑making authority, causing bottlenecks.
- Difficulty onboarding new resources.
- Increased risk of missed deadlines and budget overruns.
These issues stem from the lack of a documented baseline for scope, responsibilities, and success criteria.
