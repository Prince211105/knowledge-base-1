# Step 6 – Identify Risks, Assumptions, Issues, and Dependencies

Document known risks (e.g., short timeline, budget constraints), assumptions (e.g., availability of third‑party data), issues (current problems), and dependencies (external deliverables). Provide a mitigation or contingency plan for every risk. This proactive view helps the team anticipate obstacles and manage them before they affect the schedule.

## Can Answer

- What items should I include when documenting risks in a PID?
- How can I capture assumptions, current issues, and dependencies within a PID?

## Examples

- Risk: Limited API access – Mitigation: Early stakeholder meeting to secure access.

## Warnings

- Ignoring high‑impact risks can derail the project later.
