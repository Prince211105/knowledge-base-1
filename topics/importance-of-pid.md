# Why a Project Initiation Document (PID) Is Essential

A PID provides a solid foundation for any project, regardless of size. It aligns the team around objectives, clarifies expectations, and creates a reference point for decision‑making. By documenting scope, constraints, and success metrics early, the PID reduces the risk of miscommunication, scope creep, and rework. It also acts as a safeguard when resources change or new members join, allowing quick ramp‑up without losing project context.
