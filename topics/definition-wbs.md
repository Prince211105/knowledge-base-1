# Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) Definition

A Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) is a hierarchical decomposition of the total scope of work to be carried out by the project team to accomplish the project objectives and create the required deliverables (PMI, 2013, p. 126). It includes every deliverable that must be produced for the final product, service, or process, as well as any intermediary methodology deliverables required to manage the project effort. The WBS is built in layers of granularity, with child layers clarifying the deliverables of their parent levels.
