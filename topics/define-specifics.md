# Define Project Scope, Requirements, and Deliverables

Specifics include a clear in‑scope and out‑of‑scope list, initial requirements, project boundaries, expected deliverables, assumptions, and constraints such as revision limits. This section clarifies what the team must produce to deem the project successful.

## Can Answer

- How can I clearly identify in‑scope versus out‑of‑scope items for a project?
- What should I include under assumptions and constraints in a Project Initiation Document?

## Examples

- In‑scope: Design of 5 landing pages. Out‑of‑scope: Ongoing SEO services.
- Assumption: Content will be provided by client within two weeks.

## Warnings

- Missing out‑of‑scope items often lead to scope creep.
