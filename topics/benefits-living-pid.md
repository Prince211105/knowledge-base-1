# Advantages of Maintaining a Living Project Initiation Document (PID) Throughout the Project Lifecycle

Because the PID is stored centrally and updated as the project evolves, it provides:
- A single source of truth for scope, success metrics, and governance.
- Quick onboarding material for new team members.
- A baseline for evaluating change requests against original assumptions.
- Documentation for audit trails and post‑mortem analysis.
Maintaining the PID reduces miscommunication and keeps the project aligned with its original intent.
