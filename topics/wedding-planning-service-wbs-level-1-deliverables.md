# Wedding planning service WBS level 1 deliverables

## Overview

Lists the seven level 1 deliverable categories in the wedding planning service example.

## Detailed Summary

The wedding planning service WBS includes seven categories of deliverables at level 1: Attire for the Wedding Party, Ceremony, Reception, Rehearsal Dinner, Bridal Lunch, Accommodations, and Transportation. These represent the major service-related deliverable groupings that could be included for a given wedding project.

## Content

In the wedding planning service example, the WBS begins with seven top-level branches: Attire for the Wedding Party, Ceremony, Reception, Rehearsal Dinner, Bridal Lunch, Accommodations, and Transportation. These categories capture the major service areas that can be provided to the customer as part of the wedding planning solution.

## Troubleshooting

## Decision Rules
