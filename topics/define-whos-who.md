# Create a Who’s Who Register and RACI Matrix

List all internal and external participants, capture their role descriptions, and specify decision‑making authority. Use a RACI chart to map each deliverable or activity to Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, and Informed parties. Ensure only one person is Accountable per deliverable to avoid confusion.

## Can Answer

- How do I document team roles and responsibilities for a project?
- What steps are needed to create a RACI chart?
- Which information belongs in the Who’s Who section of a project plan?

## Examples

- Deliverable: Final UI Mockups – Responsible: UI Designer, Accountable: Art Director, Consulted: Product Owner, Informed: Development Lead.

## Warnings

- Assigning multiple Accountable persons creates decision bottlenecks.
