# Document Team Structure and Create a RACI Matrix

List all internal and external participants, their roles, and decision‑making authority. Use a RACI chart to map each deliverable or activity to Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, and Informed parties. Ensure only one person is Accountable per deliverable to avoid confusion.

## Can Answer

- How can I document team roles and responsibilities for a project?
- What information should I include in a ‘Who’s Who’ section?
- How do I assign Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, and Informed roles in a RACI chart?

## Examples

- Deliverable: Final UI Mockups – Responsible: UI Designer, Accountable: Art Director, Consulted: Product Owner, Informed: Development Lead.

## Warnings

- Assigning multiple Accountable persons creates decision bottlenecks.
