# Understanding the RACI Chart: Roles and Benefits

R – Responsible: the person who performs the work. A – Accountable: the person who makes final decisions and signs off. C – Consulted: people whose input is required before work proceeds. I – Informed: people who need to be kept aware of progress. The RACI matrix aligns expectations, reduces miscommunication, and supports clear governance in projects.
