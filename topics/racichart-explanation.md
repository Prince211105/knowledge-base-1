# Understanding RACI Chart Roles

R – Responsible: person who performs the work. A – Accountable: person who makes final decisions and signs off. C – Consulted: people whose input is needed before work proceeds. I – Informed: people who need to be kept aware of progress. The RACI matrix aligns expectations and reduces miscommunication.
