# Step 7: Distribute the Project Initiation Document and Secure Stakeholder Sign‑off

Once the PID is finalized:
1. Share the document with the entire project team, including new members who join later.
2. Circulate it to external stakeholders (client, steering committee).
3. Conduct a walkthrough meeting to answer questions and confirm understanding.
4. Capture formal sign‑off (e‑signature, email confirmation) to lock in assumptions, RACI assignments, and scope.
5. Store the PID in a central, version‑controlled location for future reference.
Having a signed PID provides a contract‑like baseline for future change‑request discussions.

## Examples

- Email with attached PID, followed by a 30‑minute kickoff call and an e‑signature on the final page.

## Warnings

- Skipping formal sign‑off may leave the project vulnerable to scope disputes.
