# Including Project Management Deliverables in a Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)

In addition to solution deliverables, a WBS may include project management deliverables such as Budget, Budget Release Schedule, and Milestones. Some teams prefer a separate Project Methodology WBS that complements the solution WBS. Including these deliverables ensures that management artifacts are tracked alongside product, service, or process components, providing a comprehensive view of all work required for project execution.
