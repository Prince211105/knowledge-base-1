# Training course WBS level 1 deliverables

## Overview

Lists the level 1 deliverable categories for a project to create a training course.

## Detailed Summary

For a project initiated to create a new training course, the WBS would likely contain at least these common level 1 categories of deliverables: Training Materials, Resources, Advertising, Logistics, and Training Administration. These represent the highest-level grouping of project deliverables for the course.

## Content

In the training course example, the WBS starts at level 1 with five major deliverable categories: Training Materials, Resources, Advertising, Logistics, and Training Administration. At this level, stakeholders understand the main areas of output but do not yet know the detailed components within each category. This level establishes the high-level scope structure for the course project.

## Warnings

## Limitations

- These categories are common examples, not an absolute universal template.

## Troubleshooting

- If the level 1 categories do not explain the course scope sufficiently, continue decomposition into lower levels.

## Decision Rules

- Use level 1 only as the starting point; decompose further when stakeholders need more understanding of what will be produced.
