# Project management deliverables in or separate from the WBS

## Overview

Explains how project management deliverables may be represented alongside solution deliverables.

## Detailed Summary

While every WBS includes hierarchy legs for the final product, service, or process deliverables, it may also include hierarchy legs for project management deliverables. Some teams represent project management deliverables in a separate WBS from the project solution WBS. In the workplace relocation example, Funding includes project management deliverables such as Budget and Budget Release Schedule. A common crossover from a separate WBS is the inclusion of milestone deliverables on the specific project WBS.

## Content

A WBS can include not only solution deliverables but also project management deliverables needed to manage execution. Teams may choose to keep these management-related outputs in the same WBS or in a separate project methodology WBS. Examples include Budget and Budget Release Schedule. In practice, milestone deliverables are also sometimes shown in the project-specific WBS even when management deliverables are otherwise handled separately.

## Best Practices

- Choose a representation approach that stakeholders understand clearly.
- Be explicit about whether management deliverables are embedded or maintained in a companion WBS.

## Examples

- Budget and Budget Release Schedule are shown as funding-related deliverables in the workplace relocation WBS.

## Warnings

- Mixing management and solution deliverables without clear structure can confuse scope interpretation.

## Limitations

- The document does not prescribe a single mandatory representation style for management deliverables.

## Troubleshooting

- If stakeholders are confused about why management outputs appear in the WBS, explain whether the project uses an integrated or separate methodology WBS approach.

## Decision Rules

- Include project management deliverables in the project WBS if that improves clarity, or maintain them in a separate methodology WBS if that better suits the team.
