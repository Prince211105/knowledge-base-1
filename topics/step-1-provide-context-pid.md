# Define Project Context in a Project Initiation Document (PID)

The context section answers: why the client is pursuing the project, what problem it solves, and how it aligns with organisational strategy. Include:
- Business drivers (strategic, technical, market).
- Project purpose and problem statement.
- High‑level goals and objectives.
- Success definition and measurable metrics.
- A concise mission statement.
This information helps keep the team focused on delivering value and makes it easier to evaluate scope changes later.

## Can Answer

What details belong in the context section of a Project Initiation Document?

## Examples

- Business driver: Reduce manual processing time by 30%.
- Goal: Launch the e‑commerce platform by Q3 2024.

## Warnings

- Avoid vague statements; ensure each driver links to measurable outcomes.
